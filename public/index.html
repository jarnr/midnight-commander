About the Game

Defend Earth in Midnight Commander, the ground-breaking 3D action game that catapults you into the heart of space warfare! Pilot US military's most advanced spacecraft and confront cunning alien invaders amidst stunning cosmic vistas.

Take Command

Outflank enemies, manage resources, and utilize high-tech weapons in epic zero-gravity battles. With a gripping narrative and strategic gameplay, the fate of the universe is in your hands. Strap in, take command, and let the space saga unfold!
